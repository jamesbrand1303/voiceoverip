About Us

Our Story

TechGuard Solutions was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide top-quality security and technology solutions to homes and businesses across South Africa. What started as a small operation with just three employees has grown into a trusted provider of CCTV security, VoIP services, and solar solutions.

Our team of certified technicians and engineers are dedicated to delivering exceptional products and services that meet the unique needs of each client. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
